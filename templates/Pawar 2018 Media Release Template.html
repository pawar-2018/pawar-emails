Illinois Gubernatorial Candidate Alderman Ameya Pawar
meeting with Chicago Area Berniecrats

CHICAGO — Ameya Pawar, 47th Ward Alderman and Democratic candidate for Illinois governor, will meet with Chicago Area members of the Local Berniecrats at the Lincoln-Belmont Library on Saturday, March 11 to discuss with voters his progressive platform, A New Deal for Illinois. Attendees are invited to learn about the details of Pawar’s vision and ask questions during a Q&A session. Those interested in attending can register on the event’s Facebook page.

Modeled in the spirit of President Franklin D. Roosevelt’s economic and social policies, which turned around our nation as it struggled to recover from the Great Depression, Pawar’s New Deal for Illinois will invigorate our state’s economy and create more opportunities for Illinoisans to get ahead.

Now, after more than 21 months without a state budget, Illinois families and businesses have lost confidence in our government’s ability to put politics aside and work together for the good of the people. As the next governor of Illinois, Ameya Pawar will bring people together and lead our state out of its current stalemate, actually solving the problems we all know we have to address.
